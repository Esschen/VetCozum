Since 2007...

Vet-Cozum Animal Hospital and Laboratory is a family business that established in 2007 by two veterinary surgeons with over 30-year experience. It provides wide range of services for small and large animals in Turkey. Consultancy services are also available for the countries outside of Turkey depending on the request.

It is located in the heart of Odemis, Izmir where offers high convenience to patient owners to commute. Vet-Cozum is open 6 days a week and operates 24/7 in case of emergency.


Fadime Orhan, Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM)

Fadime graduated in 1988 from Ankara University, Faculty of Veterinary and began her career in Ankara, Turkey. She then worked for public sector for 23 years and retired as Chief Veterinary Officer from Izmir Metropolitan Municipality in 2011. Since 2012, she has been working with his husband in their family business Vet-Cozum. As she has deep expertise in small animal practice, she leads all small animal operations in Vet-Cozum.


Osman Orhan, Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM)

Osman graduated in 1987 from Ankara University, Faculty of Veterinary and began his career in Ankara, Turkey. After he worked for private and public sector for 20 years, he found Vet-Cozum in 2007. He has been teaching in Ege University temporarily as well. He leads all large animal operations in Vet-Cozum and provides on-site/off-site services in Turkey. He also provides consultancy services across Europe with his wife.
