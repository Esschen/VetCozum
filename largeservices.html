Large Animal Services

General Consultation / Health Exams:

General consultations do not require appointment but occasionally additional time may be needed for an individual case or emergency. For this reason, we recommend you to give us a short call to secure your appointment if your schedule is tight. During an exam, we perform a “nose to tail” examination of your animal. Examinations are a great opportunity learn more about your animal’s health. The information we collect during an exam allows us to spot the signs of disease and illness and treat them before they can adversely affect your animal’s life.

Vaccinations:

Vaccinations are one of the most important parts of your animal's preventive health care plan. Many diseases can be prevented through the use of vaccinations. We provide different range of vaccinations, please visit our vaccination page here for more information.

Off-site / Farm Visits:

We provide off-site services for farms/institutions regardless of the number of animals. Please make an appointment by phone, e-mail or contact form for off-site/farm visit requests. This will allow us to prepare everything for you without any delays.


Laboratory Testing:

Laboratory tests are an essential tool in diagnosing many conditions, especially for large animals. Certain blood tests can be performed quickly and efficiently. Please reach out to us to get more information about laboratory test we perform in Vet-Cozum.

Medications / Prescription:

We stock most medications in our clinic and prescribe them anytime without any delay or additional effort on your side. Repeat prescriptions if required are made available as well. The vet will advise you on the instructions. If there is a need for long-term medication, please give us 24-hour notice by phone or e-mail so we can have the medication ready for you to collect on time.

Referrals:

Vet-Cozum delivers daily services 6 days a week and emergency services 24/7. However, from time to time, we may need to refer your request to another specialist.
